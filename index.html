Collaboration Tool Choices and Use in Remote Software Teams

Emerging Results from an Ongoing Study

Victoria Jackson, André van der Hoek - University of California, Irvine, USA
Rafael Prikladnicki - PURCS, Brazil

Motivation

Definition

Remote teams have each team member working in a different space and place than everyone else

Research Questions

  1. What tools do remote software teams use to collaborate and how do they choose which to use?
  2. How do remote software teams use tools to collaborate?
  3. What challenges do they face with their tooling?

Data Collection

  • Interview study
  • Interviews ranged from 24 to 52 minutes
  • Average length 40 minutes
